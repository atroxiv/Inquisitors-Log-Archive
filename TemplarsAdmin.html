From now on this channel will be used for logging Officer Related Punishments for only the Command/Administration to see and not at Punishment Logs. Red Cell Justice Representatives can also view this channel to ease their work for investigations and such.
Punishments that can be issued towards an Officer for breaking restrictions or abusing his privileges will be explained bellow:
Verbal Warning - The individual has committed a minor offense such as being a nuisance within events or channels and this is to remind us of his behavior for future promotions and positions. Can also increase future punishments for the individual to be harsher than normal.
Disciplinary Strike - This is a somewhat a medium punishment that can be issued if the individual committed some unacceptable such as toxicity within channels/events or has broken a regulation/restriction. Keep in mind that this strike can only be removed by the Administration if we see fit, two Disciplinary Strikes leads to an exile from the Officer Core.
Officer Exile - Pretty explanatory, it means the individual was removed from the Officer Core as a punishment for abusing his privileges such as Admin Abuse during events/within channels or for breaking high regulations and restrictions.
Suspension- The individual is unable to join the Officer-Core for a limited time period indicating that he is untrustworthy for such position at the time.
Officer Blacklist - The individual has done something unspeakable or has harm the Officer-Core or Templars in some way or form. Has deemed unfit to be ever considered the opportunity of becoming an Officer or higher within the Division.
